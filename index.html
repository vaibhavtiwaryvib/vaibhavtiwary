Vaibhav Tiwary

PhD Researcher in Aging, Epigenetics, and Mitochondrial Biology

Investigating how mitochondrial signaling, optogenetic interventions, and transgenerational epigenetic inheritance shape aging and stress resilience in Drosophila melanogaster.

About

A dedicated researcher passionate about understanding the molecular mechanisms of aging and epigenetic inheritance and developing interventions to promote healthy longevity, including plant-derived compounds as potential drugs for lifespan extension.

My research philosophy is centered on this intersection, and I believe that understanding how cellular stress responses—including those modulated by plant-derived longevity compounds—are inherited across generations will unlock new strategies for promoting healthy aging.
