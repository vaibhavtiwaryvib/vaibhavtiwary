Vaibhav Tiwary

PhD researcher studying how mitochondrial signaling, pharmacological interventions, and transgenerational epigenetic inheritance shape aging in Drosophila melanogaster.

Research & Projects

My research focuses on mitochondrial signaling, pharmacological interventions, and transgenerational epigenetic inheritance to understand aging in Drosophila melanogaster. I explore how light-activated proton pumps, drug treatments, and inherited epigenetic modifications influence lifespan, stress resilience, and behavior.

Master Thesis

Role of Polycomb (PcG) and Trithorax (TrxG) proteins in transgenerational inheritance induced by heat-stress (CSIR-CCMB, 2020).

Gene Expression in Drosophila

Study of lifespan extension in Drosophila treated with ellagic acid (VIT, 2019).

Cytotoxic Studies

Effect of bioaccessible fractions of Piper nigrum, Zingiber officinale, Phyllanthus emblica, Ocimum sanctum against MCF-7 cells (VIT, 2019).

Zebrafish Development

Effect of cobalt chloride on development of Zebrafish embryos (VIT, 2018).
