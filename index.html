About Me

A dedicated researcher passionate about understanding the molecular mechanisms of aging and developing interventions to promote healthy longevity.

Research Philosophy

My research focuses on the intersection of epigenetics, mitochondrial biology, and aging. I believe that understanding how cellular stress responses are inherited across generations will unlock new strategies for promoting healthy aging.

Currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Rostock and Leibniz Institute of Farm Animal Biology (FBN), I investigate how light-activated proton pumps can modulate cellular energetics and influence lifespan in Drosophila melanogaster.
